This Chaos Construction puzzle is named after a character in Paper Mario: The Thousand-Year Door.

Rules

Normal Irregular Sudoku rules apply, but the regions must be determined by the solver. All regions are orthogonally connected groups of 9 cells. Some region borders are given.

A digit in a cell with arrows indicates the number of cells seen in the indicated directions combined, including itself. Region borders obstruct vision.
