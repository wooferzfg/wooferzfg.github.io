Rules

Normal Irregular Sudoku rules apply, but the regions must be determined by the solver. All regions are orthogonally connected groups of 9 cells.

Draw a single non-intersecting loop that travels orthogonally through the centers of some cells which passes through each region exactly once.

A digit in a cell with arrows indicates the total number of cells in the same region as that cell that are visited by the loop, in the indicated directions combined, including itself.
