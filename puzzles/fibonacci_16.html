Normal Sudoku rules apply.

Place the following digits (the first 16 Fibonacci numbers, not including zeros) in order anywhere on the yellow cells to form a path.

Each digit on the path must be on one of the eight cells that is adjacent to the previous digit on the path.

The path is allowed to cross itself, but cells may not be repeated on the path.
