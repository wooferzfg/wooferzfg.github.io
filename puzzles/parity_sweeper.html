Rules

Normal Sudoku rules apply.

An odd digit in a diamond indicates the number of odd digits in the (up to) 9 surrounding cells, including itself.

An even digit in a diamond indicates the number of even digits in the (up to) 9 surrounding cells, including itself.

Not all diamonds are necessarily given.
